DREAMLANDS

Short Film

Duration: 15 minutes

Year: 2016

A coming-of-age story about the pressures on young women in a hyper-sexualised, image-obsessed society. The film explores themes of identity, sexuality, and self-discovery through the eyes of its teenage protagonists.
