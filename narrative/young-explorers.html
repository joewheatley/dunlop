THE YOUNG EXPLORERS

Short Film

Duration: 15 minutes

Year: 2016

A coming-of-age story that follows a group of teenagers as they navigate the complexities of friendship, identity, and self-discovery during a summer adventure. The film captures the raw emotions and transformative moments of youth.
