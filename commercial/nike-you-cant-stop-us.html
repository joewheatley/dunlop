NIKE: YOU CAN'T STOP US

Commercial

Client: Nike

Agency: Wieden+Kennedy

Year: 2020

A powerful campaign celebrating the unifying power of sport and human resilience. The film showcases athletes from all walks of life, demonstrating that nothing can stop the human spirit when we come together.
