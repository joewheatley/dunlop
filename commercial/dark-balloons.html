DARK BALLOONS

Commercial

Client: Volkswagen

Agency: DDB Paris

Year: 2022

A haunting and visually striking piece that uses the metaphor of dark balloons to explore themes of environmental responsibility and the automotive industry's role in shaping a sustainable future.
