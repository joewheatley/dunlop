BILLBOARDS

Commercial

Client: Billboards

Agency: Mother London

Year: 2019

A whimsical and innovative campaign that brings static billboards to life through magical realism. The film challenges our perception of traditional advertising spaces while creating moments of unexpected joy and wonder.
